What is

The hardships faced during the admission processes at the Higher Educational Institutes forms the cornerstone of our project "SMARTMISSION". This profound system can be used in admission process. Machine Learning forms the cognitive basis of this system. The indeginous python framework, Django made it possible to collaborate the backend with the front end. The machine learning model built inside takes on previous academic records of the student and gives a coherent prediction of the performance of the student in his/her undergraduate program. The predicted results forms the factor of deciding whether to admit the student or not, whose judicial consent and sovereignty lies only in the hands of the deans who also are the end users of the system. Thus the system not only makes the admission process easier but also preemptive decisions and the users beneficiaries.
